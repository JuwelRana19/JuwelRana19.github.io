Academic Award and Scholarship

Honours and funding for study

  • 2023–2026

    Fonds de recherche du Québec — Santé (FRQS) for doctoral training in Epidemiology at the Department of Epidemiology, Biostatistics and Occupational Health, McGill University, Canada.

  • 2022–2023

    Alma Mater Fellowship (internal award), Faculty of Medicine, and Health Sciences Studentship Award for doctoral study in Epidemiology at the Department of Epidemiology, Biostatistics and Occupational Health, McGill University, Canada.

  • 2021–2022

    Graduate Excellence Award for doctoral study in Epidemiology at the Department of Epidemiology, Biostatistics and Occupational Health, McGill University, Canada.

  • 2017–2019

    Erasmus Mundus Excellence Scholarship by the European Union for the double master’s degree in European Public Health at the University of Sheffield, UK, and École des hautes études en santé publique (EHESP), France.

  • 2017–2019

    Erasmus Mundus Excellence Scholarship by the European Union (declined) for the joint master’s degree in Public Health in Disasters by the Universidad de Oviedo, Spain, and Karolinska Institutet, Sweden.

  • July 2012

    Freedom Fighter Sheikh Kamal Scholarship for two consecutive semesters at the MSS for excellence in the MSS in Sociology program.

  • July 2011

    Dhaka University Merit Scholarship for two consecutive semesters at the MSS for excellence in the BSS in Sociology program.

  • 2008–2013

    Exim Bank Merit Scholarship for eight consecutive semesters at the BSS and two semesters at the MSS in Sociology, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

  • 2009–2013

    Structural Engineers Ltd. (SEL) Charitable Foundation Merit Scholarship for eight consecutive semesters at the undergraduate level and two semesters at the MSS, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

  • 2007–2012

    HSC Education Board Merit Scholarship based on the GPA at the Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) for eight consecutive semesters of BSS at the University of Dhaka from the Rajshahi Education Board, Bangladesh.
