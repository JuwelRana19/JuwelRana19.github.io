Biography

Scientific Award

Recognition for research contributions

  • 2025

    Best Poster Award (1st Place) at the 2025 SPGH Research and Public Health Day, McGill University, Canada. Certificate of recognition.

  • 2024–2025

    Outreach Project Leader in Global Health as the Editor-in-Chief of McGill Journal of Global Health, funded by the McGill Global Health Program, McGill University, Canada.

  • 2021

    Paul Dudley White International Scholar Award for one of the highest-ranked abstracts on artificial neural network models for lipid profile prediction in the US general population at the Annual Scientific Meeting on Cardiovascular Diseases by the American Heart Association. Web app: https://jrana.shinyapps.io/lipidr/

  • 2021

    Paul Dudley White International Scholar Award for one of the highest-ranked abstracts on explaining the increase of hypertension prevalence in Bangladesh from 2011 to 2018: evidence from national surveys in the Annual Hypertension Scientific Meeting of the American Heart Association.

  • 2021

    One-year International Society for Environmental Epidemiology (ISEE) Membership Award by the ISEE Asia and Western Pacific Chapter.

  • 2015

    Best Poster Award (3rd Place) at The Universal Health Coverage: A Policy & Solutions Conference organized by PPRC, The Rockefeller Foundation, The World Bank, and other partners in Bangladesh.

  • 2013

    MSS Dissertation Award from the Bangladesh Institute of Labour Studies (BILS) and Manusher Jonno Foundation, Bangladesh.
