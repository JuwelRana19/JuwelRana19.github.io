Academic training and professional development

Academic Training

  • 2021–25: PhD in Epidemiology, Department of Epidemiology, Biostatistics and Occupational Health, Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences, McGill University, Canada. Concentration: Environmental Epidemiology.

  • 2018–19: International Master of Public Health with specialization in Environmental and Occupational Health Sciences, EHESP School of Public Health, France.

  • 2017–18: Master of European Public Health, University of Sheffield, United Kingdom.

  • 2012–13: Master of Social Sciences in Sociology, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

  • 2008–11: Bachelor of Social Sciences (Honours) in Sociology, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Before joining the Department of Public Health at North South University, he worked as a J1 Research Scholar at the Department of Biostatistics and Epidemiology, University of Massachusetts Amherst, USA.
