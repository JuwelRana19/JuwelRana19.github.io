Biography

Research and Travel Grants

Project funding and travel support

  • 2024

    IDRC Travel Grants (No. 109856-001) from the International Development Research Centre (IDRC) to attend and share findings and outcomes from KIX-supported knowledge exchange and applied research at the Comparative International Education Society’s annual conference (CIES 2024) in Miami, Florida, USA, from 10–14 March 2024.

  • 2023–2026

    Research Grant from the Shanghai Pujiang Programme as Co-Principal Investigator: the relationship between environmental quality improvement and health outcomes in China and North America, in collaboration with East China University of Science and Technology, China.

  • June 2023

    GREAT Travel Award, McGill University, Canada, to attend the Society for Epidemiologic Research Conference in Portland, USA.

  • 2021–2024

    Research Grant as Co-Principal Investigator (Project ID 109655): Effectiveness and Scalability of Programs for Children Who Are Out of School and at Risk of Dropping Out in Bangladesh, Bhutan, and Nepal, funded by the Knowledge and Innovation Exchange (KIX) Program of the International Development Research Centre (IDRC), Canada.

  • 2020–2021

    Faculty Research Grant awarded by North South University, Division for Conference Travel and Research Grants (CTRG), for research on the impact of food insecurity on health status, suicidal ideation, and quality of life among adults living in poverty: a study in urban slums of Bangladesh.

  • 2021

    Conference bursary (registration and workshop) to present at the World Congress of Epidemiology hosted by the International Epidemiological Association.

  • 2020

    Conference bursary (virtual registration) to present at the International Society for Environmental Epidemiology Annual Congress in August 2020.

  • 2019

    J1 Scholar (February–December 2019) at the Department of Biostatistics and Epidemiology, School of Public Health and Health Sciences, University of Massachusetts Amherst, USA.

  • 2019

    Conference bursary (travel, accommodation, and registration) to present at the Joint Annual Scientific Meeting of the Society for Social Medicine & Population Health and the International Epidemiological Association, 4–6 September 2019, University College Cork, Ireland.
